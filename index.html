The Power of Multiculturalism

Culture in Class is an action campaign that aims to end the segregation of ethnic cultures in class. Our goal is to encourage a multicultural community to all audiences, as everyone plays an important role in recognizing diversity.

Join us in creating a world where cultural differences are acknowledged and celebrated.

Unity

Unity is one of our key values in Culture In Class. We believe that multicultural classrooms can create unified classrooms that stand up against the preconceptions of race and ethnicity.

Education

We believe that every student, regardless of race or ethnicity, is entitled to an equal, bias-free education. Every student should learn about history with an impartial narrative.

Culture

We believe that every classroom should be welcoming to all individuals, regardless of race, nationality or ethnic origin. Students will learn to be proud of their own heritage and will be more accepting of others.
